Class 4 Math Puzzle

Move two matchsticks to make 4 triangles

First see the starting figure, then try the interactive puzzle. The trick is to stop thinking flat and imagine the sticks folding into a 3D shape.

Original figure 9 matchsticks

Interactive Puzzle

Click the 2 glowing sticks

Click a glowing stick to move it into place.

Click stick 1 and stick 2. Each one jumps into the correct position.

Answer: make a triangular pyramid

The 4 triangles appear when the puzzle is viewed in 3D as a tetrahedron. The two moved sticks help complete the pyramid.

Second Puzzle

Remove 4 matchsticks to leave only 3 triangles

Click the 4 glowing sticks to remove them.

Original figure 13 matchsticks
Interactive board Remove 4

Click the glowing sticks to remove them. When 4 are gone, only 3 triangles should remain.

Answer: 3 triangles left

Remove the 4 highlighted sticks. The faded lines disappear, and the remaining figure clearly leaves only 3 triangles.
